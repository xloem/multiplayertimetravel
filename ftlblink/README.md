# FTL Blink

~ dRaFt ReAdMe ~

FTL Blink is a simpler design than multiplayer time travel, focused on providing true instantaneous transport, and on a possible physical tech demo rather than a video game.

It is, hence, much much less wise a choice to pursue, and seems much more supported! You could become an immortal trillionaire teleporting people around with disgusting and gross prototypes, superpowering medical research (and most other fields) like a rocket, right before your clients' natural death in this world!

But I thought we'd see if we could do it software-only and if that seems fun, make some ftlblink _robots_ to showcase FTL travel of physical objects.
